Future Investors of America

Who We Are

Future Investors of America, also known as FIA, is a student-led 501(c)3 nonprofit organization comprised of 400 members within the U.S. and internationally. Our goal is to expose high school students to personal finance and investing through events, workshops, clubs, and social media. We are partnered with Bumper Investing, the #1 investing platform designed for teens, to bring out members exclusive resources.

Our Goals

  • Education

  • At FIA, we have many events and workshops throughout the year with the goal of exposing high school students to personal finance and investing.

  • Experience

  • We allow high school students to gain realistic investing experience through numerous investment competititions and opportunities.

  • Commmunity

  • We hope to build a strong and connected community of high school students that are interested in personal finance and investing. We boast a member base of over 400 people across the US and internationally.
